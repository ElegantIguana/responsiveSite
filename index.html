Bio

Armand Guim is an junior developer in training who shares a passion in programming and game design. Born in a supremely hispanic subarb in Miami, Florida, he initially had plans of being a doctor or lawyer. Before leaving High School, he realized he could study whatever he wanted and began his foray into computer coding and technology. He still lives in Miami, working a retail job and studying at UM to become a web developer.
